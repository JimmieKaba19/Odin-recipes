Crispy Fried Cabbage

Crispy fried Cabbage shows you how to prepare finger licking crispy Cabbage that you can enjoy with your family.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the cooking oil in a pan and let if heat up
  2. Squeeze and add the garlic to the pan
  3. Add water to the pan carefully
  4. Add the rice to cook and simmer
